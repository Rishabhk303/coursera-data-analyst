Creating Visualizations using Excel for a chain of car dealerships to understand car sales and profits for Each dealer
  Created visualizations for the following captured Key Performance Indicator metrics :- 
   'Sum of Profits' for 'Hudson model cars' by 'Dealer ID' as a line chart. 
   'Profit' by 'Year' and 'Dealer ID' as a column chart. 
   Quantity Sold' by 'Dealer ID' as a bar chart.
